## ABBYY FlexiCapture enables banks to process inbound loan applications and supporting documentation related to the Paycheck Protection Program (PPP)

Banks are seeing a massive influx of PPP loan applications, which requires them to make rapid lending decisions and respond to this unprecedented volume of request from small businesses.

ABBYY FlexiCapture AI-powered intelligent document processing is perfectly designed to support the PPP application process, enabling banks to process inbound loan applications and supporting documentation from small businesses.

ABBYY FlexiCapture can be quickly deployed and works in combination with Blue Prism, and other automation platforms.

#### Get started today and learn how you can benefit.

* Eliminate manual processing of PPP loan applications (and other related documents) received from any source scan, email, or mobile
* Works with your Blue Prism robots to further validate the information and connect into your banking systems
* Once data is extracted and validated, application data is processed by the Blue Prism into the electronic loan system and delivers supporting documents to preferred repository
* Streamlines the customer experience by speeding up and ensuring accuracy of the loan process
